Some mod authors don't allow their mods to be redistributed outside of %#EB622B%CurseForge%#%, so I couldn't include them by default in the %#1BD96A%Modrinth%#% release of A Distant Journey.
%#999999%Since those mods are necessary for the pack, you will have to manually download them and add them to the `mods` folder of your instance.%#%
Clicking the buttons below will automatically prompt you to visit CurseForge and download the correct version of the needed mods. All you will need to do is **move them to the right place**!
