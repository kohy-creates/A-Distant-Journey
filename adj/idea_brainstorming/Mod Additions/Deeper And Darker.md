# Deeper And Darker

I got asked to include it back in like October/November 2025 as it was the winning feature of the vote I held after winning the CurseForge Future Mod Jam. I intalled it to check if the features would fit into the modpack and I immediately disliked the mod due to how empty it was. Like it legit has 0 outstanding features whatsoever...

Item/Block reference: [HERE](https://www.minecraft-guides.com/mod/deeper-and-darker/items/).

------
## Deep Dark Generation Rehaul

My first recent idea to incorporate it was to remove the Otherside dimension completely and instead integrate all of the blocks found there into regular Deep Dark generation.

- Replace Stone/Deepslate with Sculk Stone in generation, with Gloomslate replacing those in the lowest layers of the world. 
- All ores would be replaced with the special stone variants included in the mod.
- The weird temple structure would be moved to the overworld structure pool, spawning in Deep Dark biomes (duh).
- Tree variants from The Otherside would generate inside Deep Darks like e.g. Azalea Trees in Lush Caves.
	- Perhaps they could be used even more like Azalea Trees? Like appearing on the surface to indicate a Deep Dark biome below. I could do something fancy with feature stacking, cause Lithostitched allows that on 1.20.1.
- The unique plants growing in The Otherside would be moved to Deep Dark biomes. 
	- Sculk Tendrils would grow next to Water/Lava aquifers.
	- Sculk Vines would grow anywhere randomly in the biome.
## Items

- Heart of The Deep would be kept as a Warden drop, but would no longer be used to activate portals to The Otherside. Likely would become a part of the recipe for Eye of Curiosity.
- Soul Crystals would no longer be dropped by The Stalkers, instead they would likely become some Create crafting tree Their only purpose would still be crafting Soul Elytras.
