# Sculk Horde

I had an idea a while back ago to add an infection to Chapter 3+, similarly to how Crimson/Corruption and Hallow spawn in Hardmode in Terraria.

When initially deciding on the idea, I ask myself "What mod would be the best?". This was tricky cause I would have to choose both something aesthetically pleasing, something that I can tie into the lore of the modpack WITHOUT just saying `Yeah uhhh the Prism made it :3` (because that cannot be my get-out-of-jail-free card for *everything*) AND something that performs smoothly enough on worse machines.

I gave Sculk Horde a quick glance to see if I could integrate it in my own way into the modpack, and I think the answer is pretty much yes...? Idk, I didn't test it extensively, or in fact, I didn't test it at all. This is just wild brainstorming.

---
## The Start of The Infection

As previously mentioned, the infection would start in Chapter 3, since it is what I consider the ADJ's equivalent of Hardmode.

In Sculk Horde, the infection starts when a player interacts with some Sculk Orb Entity Thing™ that initiates the spread and becomes the first infection node. In ADJ, it would just probably choose a random spot in the world about 4k blocks away from the world center (on 20k x 20k worlds, possibly much closer in smaller worlds).

I would have to find a way to spawn the node entity, make sure it can't be just wiped with the right equipment (if it is even possible to kill it in SH in the first place), and both of those are easier said than done. Like I barely grasped how force loading chunks works in data packs and now I'd need to do it in KubeJS.

## Treasures From The Infection

Of course the new Sculk infection biome would come with its own set of gear, but I am still unsure on it, especially since 1.4.0 already gave plenty of gear alternatives in Chapter 3. I suppose a little bit more wouldn't hurt, but I'm afraid of the game becoming bloated, especially since ADJ's progression has a lot less steps than e.g. Terraria's.
